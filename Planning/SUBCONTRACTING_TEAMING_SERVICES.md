## Overview

Aliff Services provides comprehensive subcontracting and teaming services to help businesses enter and succeed in government contracting markets through strategic partnerships.

### The Challenge We Solve

**For New Federal Contractors:**
- No past performance = Can't compete as prime
- Don't know which primes need their capabilities
- Struggle to find teaming opportunities
- Unclear how to transition from commercial to federal

**For Prime Contractors:**
- Need qualified small business subcontractors
- Must meet socio-economic requirements
- Limited time to vet potential partners
- Need reliable teammates with specific capabilities

**For Experienced Contractors:**
- Want to expand into new agencies/contract vehicles
- Need teaming partners for large procurements
- Seeking joint venture opportunities

## Strategic Value

### Why Subcontracting Matters

**Path to Federal Success:**
- Subcontracting builds the past performance needed to compete as prime
- Allows companies to prove capabilities on federal contracts
- Provides federal contract experience and references
- Creates relationships with agencies and primes
- Generates revenue while building credentials

**For the Federal Market:**
- 40-50% of federal contract dollars flow to subcontractors
- Large contracts often require small business subcontracting plans
- Agencies have small business goals that primes help meet
- Teaming is essential for complex, multi-disciplinary programs

**Market Size:**
- $150B+ annually in federal subcontracting
- Growing emphasis on small business participation
- Major contract vehicles require robust subcontracting
