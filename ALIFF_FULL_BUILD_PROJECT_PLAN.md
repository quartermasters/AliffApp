## Risk Management

### Technical Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| GPT-5 API not available | Medium | Medium | Use GPT-4 Turbo initially |
| Vector DB performance issues | Low | High | Load test early, choose proven solution |
| Document parsing accuracy | Medium | High | Manual review pipeline, continuous improvement |
| AI hallucinations | High | High | Human-in-the-loop, confidence thresholds |
| API cost overruns | Medium | Medium | Cost monitoring, rate limiting |

### Business Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Strategy leak to competitors | Low | Critical | Multi-layer filtering, audit logs |
| Client data breach | Low | Critical | Encryption, isolation, security audits |
| Over-reliance on AI | Medium | Medium | Human oversight for critical decisions |
| Scope creep | High | Medium | Strict sprint planning, feature freeze |
| Timeline slippage | Medium | Medium | Buffer time, weekly reviews |

### Mitigation Strategies

1. **Weekly Sprint Reviews**: Catch issues early
2. **Automated Testing**: CI/CD pipeline
3. **Security Audits**: Monthly security reviews
4. **Performance Monitoring**: Real-time alerts
5. **Backup Plans**: Fallback to manual processes
6. **Documentation**: Comprehensive docs for continuity
